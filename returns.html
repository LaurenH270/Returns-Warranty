If you are not happy with your socks, we are more than happy to take them back for a refund or exchange. If you have any questions, our Customer Service team is here to help!

Return Policy:

  • Socks must have been purchased from SockwellUSA.com within the past 60 days.
  • Socks purchased as closeout are not eligible for returns.
  • You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item
  • Exchanges through the portal must be for the same style but can be swapped for a different size or color

Submit a
Warranty Claim

If you are experiencing issues regarding the quality of your Sockwell socks either purchased from sockwellusa.com or one of our retail partners please submit a warranty claim

Defect examples include, but are not limited to:

  • Item arrived with a hole or tear
  • Premature wear
  • Size and fit issues

Our warranty team will respond to you within 1-2 business days to help resolve your issue

Commonly asked Return questions:

Q: Do you offer free returns?
A: We do not offer free returns. Postage can be purchased through our return portal or you can choose to ship using your the carrier of your choice
Q: Can I exchange for a different style?
A: Yes, but this cannot be done through out return portal. If you would like to exchange your socks for a different style please reach out to our Customer Service team and we can assist you with your request
Q: Can I return close out items?
A: Only in-season items may be returned. Closeout items cannot be returned for a refund or exchange, but may be eligible for a warranty exchange in the case of defect or damage

Commonly asked product questions:

Q: There are threads inside my sock, is this normal?
A: These threads are where the reinforcing yarn is cut but it does not affect the quality of the socks. They can be snipped off if desired
Q: My socks are different lengths when laying flat, will they fit differently?
A: Many of our socks are will vary in height/length when lying flat. This is due to the difference in the amount of float stitches, yarn and boarding time for each style. However, when you put the socks on they should stretch to fit same as the other styles
Q: How long should my socks last?
A: Our socks do not have a set lifespan due to the many factors that can affect how quickly they wear out, such as the wearer's gait, laundering frequency, choice of footwear, and how often they are worn. If you feel that your socks are wearing out prematurely or if you believe you did not get enough wear out of them, please sumbit a warranty claim
